About Us

We help students decide on the right strand for Senior High School.

Our recommendation system helps students choose the most suitable strand for Senior High School. By analyzing their interests and strengths, we provide personalized suggestions to guide them in making informed decisions about their academic path.

Our Mission is to empower students by providing a reliable recommendation system that guides them in choosing the right Senior High School strand based on their preferences, interests and skills.

Our Vision is to become a trusted platform that supports every Filipino student in making informed and confident educational decisions.
